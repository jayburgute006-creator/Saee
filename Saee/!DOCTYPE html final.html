Happy Birthday Saee!

Happy Birthday Saee! It still feels funny that the girl who was my best friend for almost 5 years is now my girlfriend.

I still remember our tuition days when we used to sit together, I would pull your T-shirt just to irritate you and we would secretly tickle each other with our legs during lectures and try not to laugh. Those small, silly moments are some of my favorite memories.

Our truth and dare games in tuition, our "Pagal Khana" group with Anushree and being Doctor and giving injections, and the Imagica trip bus you were pressing our legs were some really fun times. I also remember our 7th standard chats till midnight when I used to flirt a lot and you would say we should delete the chats or we are dead if someone sees them.

I'll never forget the day you cried on my shoulder when you found out you had to leave, and you said you would take me with you in the boot of your car.

And after 2 years when we met again on the Choti Mothi trip, it felt really special - especially that morning when we were sitting behind the tent and just talking.

I still can't believe you proposed to me. After all these years of friendship, that moment made everything even more special.

I really miss you too much sometimes, but I can't wait for the day we meet again and make more memories together. Thank you for being part of my life. I feel really lucky that the person who understood me the most for so many years is now the person I love the most.

Happyyyy Birthdayyyyyy ❤️
